Features

Capabilities built for teams that need reliable knowledge, not another content silo.

Admont AI combines authoring, retrieval, review, and reuse in one workflow. The point is not flashy AI output. The point is making institutional knowledge easier to create, easier to trust, and easier to reuse across the business.

  • Portable Markdown-based knowledge
  • AI and human review in the same loop
  • Assistant-ready retrieval workflows
  • Built for shared team operations

What this enables

  • Move faster on drafting, research, and internal reporting.
  • Keep knowledge reusable across teams and assistant workflows.
  • Turn approved content into better future answers and outputs.

Core Features

Everything serves the same goal: useful knowledge that compounds.

AI-assisted drafting

Create first drafts, rewrites, and expansions faster by grounding model output in existing documentation instead of writing every page manually from scratch.

Retrieval over your own corpus

Bring the most relevant internal knowledge into answers and writing flows so assistants work from your context, not generic public assumptions.

Markdown as the system of record

Keep published knowledge in a format your team can read, move, diff, back up, and reuse outside Admont.

Reviewable team workflow

Treat documentation as a shared business asset with history, approval, revision, and collaborative iteration instead of isolated prompt output.

Assistant access through MCP

Expose the same knowledge base to external assistants through a standard integration path rather than maintaining separate silos.

Portable by design

Avoid vendor lock-in by keeping workflows inspectable and the data model approachable, which matters when tools, teams, and deployment needs change.

Business Fit

Useful across operations, support, product, and engineering.

Operations

Document procedures, policies, and recurring tasks in a form that is easy to update and easy to retrieve.

Support

Turn resolved issues and internal fixes into a reusable answer base instead of relying on tribal memory.

Product And Engineering

Carry decisions, architecture knowledge, and implementation notes forward without burying them in chat history.

Admont AI helps teams create, reuse, and operationalize company knowledge.
